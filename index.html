Executive Summary

Queen B's is a Minnesota-based food truck serving high-margin, fast-service comfort food tailored to breweries, festivals, and private events. The brand is inspired by Owenee Bethany Kruse, whose presence and energy define the Queen B identity.

Market & Positioning

  • Primary markets: Central Minnesota and Twin Cities events.
  • Core venues: Breweries, fairs, festivals, food truck rallies, and private bookings.
  • Advantage: Tight menu, fast service, strong brand story, and modular operations.

Operations

  • Mobile Food Unit licensed in Minnesota.
  • Commissary kitchen for prep, storage, and sanitation.
  • Owner-operator model with 1–2 staff during peak events.

Financial Overview

  • Startup costs: Truck, buildout, equipment, permits, insurance, initial inventory.
  • Revenue focus: High-volume events and recurring brewery partnerships.
  • Seasonality: Strong summer revenue; winter supported by catering and pop-ups.

Funding Strategy

  • Minnesota grants and rural business programs.
  • CDFI lenders (e.g., Initiative Foundation, NDC, WomenVenture).
  • DEED loan participation, SBA microloan, and optional Kiva 0% loan.
